# Ordinary pancakes

## Ingredients

3 eggs
3 dl wheat flour
25 g butter
3 dl milk

## Steps

1. Crack the eggs (both whites and yolks, but not the shells) into a bowl.
2. Whisk.
3. Slowly add the flour while whisking.
4. Melt the butter and stir it in.
5. Stir in the milk.
6. Fry the batter in a heated frying-pan.
